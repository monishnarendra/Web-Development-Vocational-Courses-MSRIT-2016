Contact

Looking to get in touch? Great! I love reader mail. I’m happy to help you get on the road, plan your trip, ease your fears, and answer any questions you might have.

However, e-mail has gotten so out of control that I first must ask you to read following guidelines before sending me a message. While I hate to restrict email, the more time I’m on it, the less time I can be out finding information to help you travel!




Before sending me an email, please read the following guidelines:


1] If you are looking for general destination information, please consult my destination guides or use the search bar on the top right of the page. Basic information on what to see, do, and how much things cost in a place can be found throughout the site. Emails that ask “what should I do in [x]?” will simply direct you back to the guides section anyways. If you have more specific questions, feel free to ask!

2] If you are e-mailing me about visa questions related to my European visa article, please contact an embassy or consulate. I can’t comment on individual situations and will not respond to your email.

3] If you wondering where you should start on planning your trip or feeling lost, use the start here link on the top right of the navigation bar. That’s the best place to begin using this website.

4] I’m not a travel agency and emails asking me to book your trip will be deleted.

5] I love itineraries but planning them is a very personal process. My advice is always to simply avoid doubling back and move in one direction! For what places to go and thing to see, only you can answer that. I don’t know what you like.

6] If you work in PR, don’t send me press releases or put me on your mailing list. Ever.

7] I do not accept guest posts and inquiries will be deleted.

8] I don’t do sponsored posts, text links, or other advertising. All inquires will be deleted.

9] Please keep all emails to two paragraphs or less.
